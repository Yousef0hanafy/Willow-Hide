Our Story

Willow & Hide was born from a love for traditional craftsmanship and the timeless appeal of leather.

Founded by Youssef, our mission is to create beautiful, functional leather goods that stand the test of time. We believe in slow fashion, quality materials, and the value of items made with human hands and heart. Each piece is meticulously crafted in our Zhraa Nacr City workshop, blending age-old techniques with a contemporary aesthetic. We are passionate about sustainability, sourcing our leathers responsibly and minimizing waste wherever possible.
